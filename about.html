Artist & Visionary

Elle Mendoza is a Kansas City based artist working across fine art and digital media. Her practice explores the intersection of emotion, observation, and personal narrative, often using texture, form, and subtle symbolism to give shape to experiences that are difficult to articulate.

With a background in fine arts and digital media, Elle works fluidly between traditional and digital processes, allowing experimentation and intuition to guide her work. Her pieces are rooted in careful observation and hands-on making, inviting viewers to slow down and engage with the work on an emotional and sensory level.
